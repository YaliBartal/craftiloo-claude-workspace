## Key Competitive Insights

### CraftLab
- **Strongest competitor in premium kids craft kits**
- Parents Picks Award recognition builds trust
- Strong packaging and presentation
- Higher price point justified by quality perception

### KRAFUN
- **Broadest product range across categories**
- "My First" beginner positioning
- Good value-to-quality ratio
- Strong Amazon presence

### Perler (fuse beads)
- **Dominates fuse beads with licensed characters**
- Legacy brand recognition
- Available everywhere (Target, Walmart, craft stores)

### LatchKits (PlayMonster)
- **Owns the latch hook kids market**
- Retail distribution advantage
- Toy company backing

### Melissa & Doug
- **Dominates lacing cards and educational toys**
- Strong retail presence
- Premium brand perception
