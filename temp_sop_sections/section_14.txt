## 13. Experimental Campaign Testing
Frequency: Launch 1-2 new test campaigns per week
Test categories:
- Category targeting (identify new audience segments)
- Competitor ASIN targeting (steal traffic)
- New keyword themes (expand niche coverage)
- Sponsored Brand Video (visual engagement)
Evaluation period: 14 days minimum
Decision criteria:
- Scale if: ACOS <35% and CVR >8%
- Pause if: ACOS >60% and CVR <5%
- Optimize if: Results are mixed but show potential
