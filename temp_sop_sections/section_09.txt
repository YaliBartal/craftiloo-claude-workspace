## 8. Search Term Optimization (Ongoing)
### 8.1 Negative Keyword Harvesting
Frequency: Every 2-3 days minimum
Criteria for adding negatives:
- No conversions after 20+ clicks
- Irrelevant intent (clear mismatch)
- ACOS >3x target with no ranking benefit
Process:
- Review search term reports for Auto and Broad campaigns
- Add negatives at campaign level for broad exclusions
- Add negatives at ad group level for specific exclusions
- Cross-reference negatives across portfolio to avoid duplication
### 8.2 Search Term Promotion
When to promote a search term to its own SK campaign:
- 3+ orders with ACOS <40%
- Clear buying intent
- Keyword not already targeted in an exact campaign
Process:
- Create new SK campaign with promoted term
- Add original search term as negative in discovery campaign (Auto/Broad)
- Monitor for 7 days before scaling budget
