## 1. Daily Operating Routine (Baseline Control)
### 1.1 Business Reality Check – Sellerboard
Before touching PPC:
- Review total sales vs ad spend
- Identify product-level performance
- Evaluate spend efficiency by ASIN
- Track BSR movement
- Note active deals, coupons, or promotions
Purpose:
Validate whether PPC behavior aligns with business performance. Identify external factors affecting conversion or velocity.
