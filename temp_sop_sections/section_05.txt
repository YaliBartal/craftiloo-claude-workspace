## 4. Portfolio Lifecycle Stages & Campaign Structure
Each portfolio progresses through clearly defined stages. Campaigns are introduced according to intent, data reliability, and ranking maturity.
### Stage 1 – Launch: Initial Traffic
Outcome: Organic Ranking Foundation
Primary KPI: Rank movement
Profitability: Not required
(Refer to Phase 1 in the visual)
Purpose:
- Establish initial keyword visibility
- Generate conversion data
- Identify high-intent search terms
Core Campaign Types:
- SP – Exact (Single Keyword)
- SP – Exact (Halo Keywords)
- SP – Broad (Single Keyword)
- Auto – Close Match
- SP – Phrase (Single Keyword)
Operating Rules:
- Focus on exact and near-exact intent
- ACOS tolerance is high
- Budget is used to force visibility
- Ranking velocity matters more than efficiency
### Stage 2 – Launch: Targeted Traffic
Outcome: Indirect Ranking Benefit & Conversion Signal
Primary KPI: CVR + Rank stability
(Refer to Phase 2 in the visual)
Purpose:
- Expand reach while maintaining relevance
- Validate keyword intent
- Feed data into future scaling campaigns
Core Campaign Types:
- SP – Product Targeting
- SP – Broad Brand
- Auto – Substitutes
- Auto – Complements
- SP – Phrase (Your Brand + KW)
- SP – PT vs Your ASIN
- SP – PT Gap Competitors
- SP – Phrase Competitor Brands (High CV)
Operating Rules:
- Still ranking-oriented
- Spend is allowed to explore
- Begin filtering obvious waste
- Conversion data becomes more important
### Stage 3 – Scale: Maintain Profitability & Brand Defense
Outcome: Cost-Effective Traffic
Primary KPI: ACOS + Rank Retention
(Refer to Phase 3 in the visual)
Purpose:
- Defend rankings already achieved
- Extract profit from validated keywords
- Reduce inefficiency
Core Campaign Types:
- SP – Broad (Brand Defense)
- SB – Video (Exact / Phrase)
- SP – PT (Your ASIN)
- Auto – Loose
- SD – Purchase Remarketing
- SD – View Remarketing
- Placement Split Testing (TOS focus)
Operating Rules:
- ACOS discipline is enforced
- Budget is reallocated to winners
- Weak keywords are paused or isolated
- Brand defense becomes mandatory
### Stage 4 – Scale: Niche Domination & Reach
Outcome: Top-of-Funnel Control
Primary KPI: Impression Share + Market Coverage
(Refer to Phase 4 in the visual)
Purpose:
- Control the niche narrative
- Expand reach beyond direct intent
- Reinforce brand presence
Core Campaign Types:
- SB – Headline
- SB – Audience
- SD – CPM
- DSP
- SP – TV Ads (if applicable)
Operating Rules:
- Data is less precise
- Spend is strategic, not reactive
- These campaigns support dominance, not ranking discovery
