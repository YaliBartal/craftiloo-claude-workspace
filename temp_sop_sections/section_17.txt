## 16. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
### 16.1 Launch Stage KPIs
- Rank velocity (positions gained per week)
- Click-through rate (CTR) — indicates listing quality
- Total indexed keywords
- Hero keyword positions (target: top 20 within 30 days)
### 16.2 Scale Stage KPIs
- ACOS (target: 28-32%)
- TACoS (target: 15-20%)
- Conversion rate (target: 10%+)
- Impression share on hero keywords
- Market share vs top 3 competitors
### 16.3 Red Flag Metrics
- CVR <5% (listing or targeting issue)
- ACOS >50% for 7+ days (spending into the void)
- BSR declining despite increased ad spend (market saturation or poor product-market fit)
- Hero keywords dropping 10+ positions (competitor attack or algo change)
