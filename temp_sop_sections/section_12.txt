## 11. Budget Allocation Strategy
### 11.1 Portfolio-Level Budget
Launch Stage:
- Prioritize SK and Exact campaigns
- Accept higher daily spend volatility
Scale Stage:
- Allocate 30-40% of total PPC budget
- Focus on efficiency and defense
- Stabilize daily spend
### 11.2 Campaign-Level Budget
High Priority Campaigns (SK, Exact Halo):
- Set budget 2-3x average daily spend
- Never let these campaigns run out of budget
Discovery Campaigns (Auto, Broad):
- Set budget 1.5x average daily spend
- Allow these to cap out if needed
Defense Campaigns (Shield, Brand):
- Set budget high enough to never cap
- These must always be active
