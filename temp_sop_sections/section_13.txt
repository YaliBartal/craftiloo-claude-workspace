## 12. Shield Campaign Strategy
Purpose: Prevent competitors from appearing on your branded search terms and product pages.
Structure:
- Located in dedicated "Shield All" portfolio
- Targets your own brand name + product keywords
- Targets your own ASINs (product targeting)
Bidding:
- Bid high enough to maintain top placement
- ACOS is irrelevant here — this is defensive spend
Keywords to include:
- Exact brand name
- Brand + product type combinations
- Common misspellings of brand name
