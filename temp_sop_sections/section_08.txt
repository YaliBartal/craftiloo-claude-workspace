## 7. Campaign Creation Rules (Critical)
### 7.1 ASIN Strategy
Always drive spend through one main ASIN.
At launch:
- Include all family ASINs
- Identify best performer
Once identified:
- Consolidate spend into that ASIN only
Ranking strength comes from concentrated traffic.
### 7.2 Bid Management
- Bid increases are applied only on TOS
- Base bids remain controlled
- Placement behavior is isolated and intentional
### 7.3 Budget Strategy
- Start with a medium budget
- Observe spend velocity, CVR, and rank movement
- Adjust only after directional clarity
### 7.4 Ad Group Naming
Ad group name must exactly match the campaign name.
