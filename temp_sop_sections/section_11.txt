## 10. Bid Adjustment Framework
### 10.1 Bid Increase Criteria
Increase bids when:
- ACOS <25% and rank stagnant or declining
- CVR >12% and impression share <50%
- Losing TOS placement to competitors on hero keywords
How much to increase:
- Strong performers: +20-30%
- Moderate performers: +10-15%
- Test increments: +5-10%
### 10.2 Bid Decrease Criteria
Decrease bids when:
- ACOS >40% for 7+ days with no rank improvement
- CVR <6% (indicates poor relevance or listing issue)
- Keyword is ranked top 5 organically (reduce paid dependency)
How much to decrease:
- Severe waste: -30-50%
- Marginal efficiency: -15-20%
### 10.3 Pause Criteria
Pause keywords when:
- 50+ clicks, 0 orders
- ACOS consistently >100% with no strategic ranking value
- Keyword intent completely misaligned with product
