## 6. Naming Convention (Strict Standard)
Format:
Portfolio Nickname – SPM/A / Campaign Type – Keyword Structure – Placement
Example:
latch hook pillow – SPM SK latch hook kits for kids TOS
Breakdown:
- Portfolio Nickname: latch hook pillow
- SPM: Sponsored Products Manual (use "SPA" for Auto campaigns)
- SK: Single Keyword (use "MK" for Multi Keyword same root)
- Keyword: latch hook kits for kids
- TOS: Top of Search focused
Naming clarity is non-negotiable. Campaign intent must be obvious without opening it.
