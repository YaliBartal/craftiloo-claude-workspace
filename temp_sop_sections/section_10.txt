## 9. Keyword Ranking Strategy (Data Dive Review)
Frequency: 2-3x per week
What to track:
- Movement on hero keywords (primary revenue drivers)
- New keywords entering top 50 organically
- Keywords competitors are gaining ground on
- Keywords where you're ranked #15-30 (opportunity zone)
Action triggers:
- Hero keyword drops 5+ positions → increase TOS bid or create dedicated SK campaign
- Competitor outranking you on 3+ hero keywords → evaluate if they're buying rank or converting better
- New keyword breaks into top 30 → create MK or SK campaign to accelerate
Tools:
- Data Dive (primary)
- Helium 10 Keyword Tracker (secondary)
- Manual ASIN reverse lookup for competitor monitoring
