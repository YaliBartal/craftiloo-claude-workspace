## 15. Reporting & Review Cadence
### 15.1 Daily (15-20 minutes)
- Sellerboard check
- Portfolio ACOS scan
- Competitor ranking spot check
### 15.2 Every 2-3 Days (30-45 minutes)
- Search term report review
- Negative keyword harvest
- Bid adjustments
### 15.3 Weekly (60-90 minutes)
- Deep portfolio analysis (week-over-week)
- Campaign structure audit
- Ranking movement review (Data Dive)
- Launch new test campaigns
### 15.4 Monthly (2-3 hours)
- Full portfolio performance review
- Identify underperforming portfolios for restructure
- Analyze TACoS trends (Total ACOS including organic)
- Competitive landscape deep dive
- Budget reallocation across portfolios
