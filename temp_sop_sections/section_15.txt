## 14. Deal Coordination with PPC
When running Lightning Deals, Coupons, or Prime Exclusive Discounts:
Before the deal:
- Increase budgets on Auto, Broad, and SK campaigns by 30-50%
- Increase TOS bids by 15-20% to maximize visibility during high-traffic period
During the deal:
- Monitor hourly if possible
- Reallocate budget to top performers
- Pause campaigns that aren't converting
After the deal:
- Return bids and budgets to baseline within 24 hours
- Analyze which campaigns benefited most from increased traffic
- Note BSR momentum gained
