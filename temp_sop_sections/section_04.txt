## 3. Continuous Competitor Awareness (Mandatory)
PPC decisions are made in the context of the competitive landscape.
For every hero keyword and core ASIN, monitor:
- Ranking position vs main competitors
- Price increases or undercutting
- Active promotions or deals
- Main image or creative changes
- New variations or bundle introductions
Key questions:
- Who is buying rank?
- Who is sacrificing margin for visibility?
- Where are we losing or gaining relative position?
PPC is used both to attack and defend market share.
