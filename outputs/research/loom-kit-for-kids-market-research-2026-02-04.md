## Market Trends & Patterns

### What Top Sellers Have in Common
1. **Complete kits** — Everything included (loom, loops, hook, instructions)
2. **Colorful rainbow loops** — 8-17 color variety
3. **Gift positioning** — "Birthday Gifts for Girls Ages 6 7 8-12"
4. **Multiple projects** — "Makes 6 potholders" or "25 projects"
5. **Storage/carrying case** — Premium kits include portable boxes
6. **"Beginner-friendly"** messaging
7. **Dual audience** — "For Kids AND Adults"

### Listing Optimization Patterns
- **Title keywords:** "Weaving Loom Kit Toys", "Arts and Crafts", "Birthday Gifts", "Girls Ages 6 7 8-12 13"
- **Key phrases in descriptions:** "DIY", "Beginner-Friendly", "Potholder", "Craft Loops"
- **Seasonal angles:** Christmas, Birthday, Valentine's gifts
- **Age targeting:** Ages 5-12 (primary), "& Adults" (secondary)

### Emerging Trends
1. **Multi-functional kits** — 2-in-1 or 6-in-1 kits combining potholders + bracelets
2. **Premium loop materials** — Cotton vs. polyester differentiation (Friendly Loom leads this)
3. **Larger loom sizes** — 10" Pro size emerging alongside standard 7"
4. **Wooden frame looms** — Tapestry-style weaving for more artistic output
5. **Themed/seasonal kits** — Christmas, Valentine's editions
6. **Bundle value** — More loops = perceived higher value
