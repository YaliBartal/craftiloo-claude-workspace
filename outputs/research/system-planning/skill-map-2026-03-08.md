## System Overview

This AI system is a full Amazon brand operations platform. Think of it as a company org chart — each **Domain** is a department with its own **Agent** (manager) and **Skills** (specialists). At the top sits a **Master Orchestrator** — the CEO layer that knows the state of the whole business and routes to the right department.

**Architecture:** Master Orchestrator → Domain Agents → Individual Skills

**Total existing skills: 24**
**Existing domains: 5**
**New domains proposed: 3 (Inventory, Fees & Reimbursement, Master Orchestrator)**

## DOMAIN 7: Fees & Reimbursement Management *(To Build)*

### What This Domain Covers

Amazon holds money that belongs to Craftiloo. Inventory gets lost, damaged, or miscounted during FBA processing. Amazon overcharges fees when product dimensions are measured incorrectly. Returns are refunded to customers but items are never returned to FBA. Professional reimbursement services charge **15-25% of recovered funds** for doing exactly this work — we can automate it.

**Key reimbursement types:**
- **Lost in warehouse** — Amazon can't locate inventory units
- **Damaged in warehouse** — Amazon damaged units during storage or processing
- **Damaged during inbound transit** — Units damaged on the way to FBA
- **Customer return discrepancy** — Customer refunded but unit not returned to FBA
- **Inbound shipment discrepancy** — Sent 100 units, Amazon received 95
- **FBA fee overcharge** — Amazon measured product dimensions incorrectly → wrong fee tier

**New SP-API report types needed** (additions to the existing MCP server):
- `GET_FBA_FULFILLMENT_INVENTORY_ADJUSTMENTS_DATA` — lost/damaged adjustments
- `GET_FBA_REIMBURSEMENTS_DATA` — what Amazon has already paid us
- `GET_FLAT_FILE_RETURNS_DATA_BY_RETURN_DATE` — return processing

# GAP ANALYSIS — Skills to Build

## Profasee Comparison (what we learned)

Profasee Ultra focuses on **Price + Advertising** as an integrated profit system. Their key insight: pricing decisions and PPC decisions cannot be made independently. A price increase may make PPC less competitive (lower CVR) which changes ACoS. A bid increase may capture higher-converting traffic that justifies a higher price point.

**What Profasee does that we don't:**
- Dynamic/AI-driven pricing optimization
- Price elasticity simulation (what happens to revenue/profit at each price point?)
- Price + PPC integrated profit calculator
- Automated price changes based on market conditions
