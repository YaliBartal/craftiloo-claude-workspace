⚡ Cross-Domain Intelligence — Signals Only the Master Orchestrator Sees

No single domain agent would catch these patterns. Only the layer that reads all state files simultaneously can connect them.

CVR drop + BSR drop, but PPC is normal
→ Listing/product issue. Run Review Analyzer before touching bids.
Market Intel + PPC + Content
Stockout risk <14 days + PPC spend scaling
→ Pause PPC on that ASIN NOW — ads for stock that can't fulfil.
Inventory + PPC
Listing suppressed + PPC still spending
→ Pause campaigns immediately, fix suppression first.
Account Health + PPC
High ACoS + high FBA fee burden per unit
→ Product may never be profitable. Fix fees or price before more spend.
Fees + PPC + Finance
IPI score dropping + excess slow-moving inventory
→ Remove stranded/slow stock before FBA storage limit is cut.
Account Health + Inventory
Competitor BSR dropped 30%+ + our BSR flat
→ Market share opening. Increase TOS bids to capture their traffic.
Market Intel + PPC
Cash flow tight (finance) + restock due in 2 weeks
→ Alert: reorder needs $X. PPC spend should be reduced to preserve cash.
Finance + Inventory + PPC
LTSF risk approaching + poor PPC on same ASIN
→ Remove from FBA and pause ads — storing a dead product at double cost.
Fees + Inventory + PPC
Reimbursement >$500 open + 30+ days unclaimed
→ Route to reimbursement-tracker immediately.
