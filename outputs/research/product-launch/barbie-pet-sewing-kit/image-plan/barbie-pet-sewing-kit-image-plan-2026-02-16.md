### Image Narrative Arc

The 7 images guide a gift-buying parent from first impression (hero shot) through practical decision-making (what's included, is it safe, is it easy) to emotional payoff (all 7 finished pets, child engaged in activity, gift-ready packaging). The buyer journey moves from "what is it?" → "can my child do this?" → "is it worth it?" → "I want to buy this."

### Copy Notes
- **Voice:** Warm, parent-confident, informative. Not cutesy. Not salesy.
- **Key phrases from the sewing kit niche reviews:** "everything included," "easy to follow," "kept her busy for hours," "she was so proud," "great gift," "no scissors needed," "safe needles"
- **Words customers naturally use:** "adorable," "cute," "proud," "busy," "focused," "loves it," "gift," "birthday," "quality," "complete"
- **Concerns to address:** Safety (needles), age appropriateness, completeness (extra supplies?), quality (thread tangling, felt tearing), instructions clarity
