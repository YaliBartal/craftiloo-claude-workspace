## Question-to-Bullet Mapping

**Must Answer:**
- "Is this good for beginners?" → Yes, step-by-step illustrated booklet
- "Are instructions easy to follow?" → Clear visual instructions, no prior sewing needed
- "Does my child need adult help?" → Designed for independent crafting with parental guidance for youngest

### Bullet 2: What's Included (Complete Kit)
**Must Answer:**
- "Does it come with everything?" → Yes, complete kit - nothing else needed
- "What's in the kit?" → Felt bag, 5-color floss, stickers, 2 buttons, 2 strings, needle, threader, booklet
- "Is there enough thread?" → 5 colors of floss included
