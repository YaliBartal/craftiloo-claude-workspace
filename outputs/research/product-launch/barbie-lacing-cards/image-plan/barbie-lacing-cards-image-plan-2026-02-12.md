### Copy Guidelines Applied

- **Voice:** Informative, factual, listing-style. Same tone as the product bullet points. Written for the adult buyer (parent/grandparent), not the child.
- **Rufus optimization:** Every text block contains indexable keywords - "lacing cards", "Barbie", "ages 3+", "fine motor skills", "mess-free", "gift-ready", "pre-punched", "complete kit"
- **No kitschy language:** No "Lace Your Way to Fun!", no "The Perfect Barbie Experience!", no "She'll Love It!" Instead: factual statements that answer questions and contain search terms.
- **Matches listing bullets:** Text mirrors the listing bullet tone and vocabulary:
  - Listing says: *"BARBIE LACING CARDS FOR KIDS"*
  - Image says: *"Complete Barbie Lacing Kit - 8 Cards, 8 Laces, Stickers"*
