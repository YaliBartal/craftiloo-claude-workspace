## Key Competitive Intelligence Takeaways

### Strengths of LatchKits
- **Dominant brand** in latch hook kits (#2 and #4 in subcategory)
- **Massive review moat** - 7,295 reviews on flagship Unicorn
- **Strong gift positioning** - frequently bought as birthday/Christmas gifts
- **Nostalgia factor** - parents/grandparents buy for kids remembering their own childhood
- **Sold by Amazon** - 1P relationship, FBA, Prime eligible
- **Wide product line** - 14+ variations across two listing families
- **Color-coded canvas** is a praised differentiator for beginners
- Multiple product videos (4 on Unicorn, 2 on Strawberry)

### Exploitable Weaknesses
1. **Yarn durability is terrible** - 24 of 25 durability mentions are NEGATIVE on Unicorn. Yarn falls apart, frays, shreds
2. **Insufficient yarn quantities** - Strawberry Pillow consistently runs out of red yarn. Recurring complaint
3. **Yarn pieces too short** - multiple reviewers say pieces are too short for adult hands
4. **Unsorted yarn bundles** - colors mixed together, requiring tedious pre-sorting
5. **Latch hook tool quality** - described as flimsy, cheap, sometimes arrives broken or missing
6. **Color matching issues** - yarn colors do not always match printed canvas pattern
7. **Limited age appeal** - marketed as 6+ but reviewers say realistically 8+ for independent use
8. **No pattern sheet** - some kits arrive without printed patterns on canvas
