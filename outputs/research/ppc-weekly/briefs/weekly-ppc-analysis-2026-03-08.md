### State of PPC Assessment

**This is the best week since tracking started.** Every major metric improved simultaneously:

- **ACoS dropped 3.3 points to 34.2%** while sales grew 4.2% — we're becoming more efficient AND growing. This is the holy grail of PPC optimization.
