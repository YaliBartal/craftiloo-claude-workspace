## ⚠️ Critical IP Warning — "Barbie" Branding

**"Barbie" is a registered trademark of Mattel, Inc.** Using "Barbie" in your product title, bullets, or ASIN without a formal licensing agreement exposes Craftiloo to:
- Amazon listing suppression / ASIN removal
- Trademark infringement notice from Mattel
- Potential legal action

### Two Paths Forward:

**Path A — Official Mattel License**
- Reach out to Mattel Licensing (licensing.mattel.com)
- Enables use of "Barbie" name, logo, and imagery
- Typically requires minimum order commitments, royalty fees (5-15%), and design approval process
- Timeline: 3-12 months. High barrier, but creates the most defensible product.

**Path B — Generic "Fashion Doll" Positioning (Recommended for Speed)**
- Position as "Fashion Doll Knitting Kit" or "Doll Sweater Knitting Loom Kit for Kids"
- Pink/fashion color palette, doll-sized patterns included
- Keywords: target "doll knitting kit", "fashion doll loom kit" — no trademark risk
- Barbie-compatible sizing without using the name
- Market within existing keyword space using Core keywords + new doll-specific terms
- Timeline: Normal product launch (8-12 weeks)
