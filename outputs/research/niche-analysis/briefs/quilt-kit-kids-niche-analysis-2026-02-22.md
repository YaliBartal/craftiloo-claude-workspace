## Executive Summary

The "quilt kit for kids" niche is a **$100K+/month market** dominated overwhelmingly by **KRAFUN (71% revenue share)** — but there's a critical finding: **Amazon blends quilt kits and sewing kits together in search results.** The actual "quilting" products (no-sew fleece quilts) are a small subset with weak competition and poor quality. Craftiloo already appears in these results (#12 subcategory) with its sewing kits. The real opportunity is a **dedicated kids quilting/patchwork product** — a category with zero quality competitors and clear customer demand for better materials. Proceed with a quilting-specific product, not another sewing kit.

## Recommendation

**Verdict: CONDITIONAL GO**

### Why GO
1. **Zero competition for kids' quilting/patchwork sewing kits** — A "My First Quilt" patchwork sewing kit would be literally the only product in this sub-niche
2. **Amazon's Choice quilt kit (Made By Me) is extremely vulnerable** — 50% of recent reviews cite terrible fabric quality, 7% dissatisfaction rate. A quality alternative would steal this position
3. **Perfect brand alignment** — Craftiloo already ranks #12 in Kids' Sewing Kits, has the supplier relationships, and serves the exact target audience (grandparent-to-kid gifts)

### Why CONDITIONAL (not full GO)
1. **KRAFUN's dominance is real** — 71% revenue share means PPC costs in "sewing kit" keywords will be high
2. **"Quilt kit" is a smaller sub-niche than "sewing kit"** — Monthly volume for quilt-specific terms is lower
3. **Need to validate quilting-specific demand** — The keyword data shows opportunity but actual search volume needs verification

### Risks
1. **KRAFUN could launch a quilt kit** — They have the brand presence and ad budget to enter quickly
2. **Seasonal demand** — Craft kits peak Q4 (holiday gifting). Need to plan launch timing for pre-holiday
3. **Material cost** — "Premium quality" positioning requires actually premium materials, which may squeeze margins

### If Proceeding

**Two product paths (can pursue either or both):**

**Path A: No-Sew Fleece Quilt Kit**
- **Positioning:** Premium alternative to Made By Me (Amazon's Choice)
- **Target price:** $19.99-$22.99
- **Differentiation:** Thick genuine fleece, gift-ready box, knot-tying guide with illustrations
- **Target keywords:** "no sew quilt kit for kids," "fleece blanket making kit," "tie blanket kit"
- **Fastest to market** — simpler product, proven demand

**Path B: "My First Quilt" Patchwork Sewing Kit**
- **Positioning:** First-ever kids' quilting sewing kit — teach real quilting
- **Target price:** $24.99-$26.99 (in the $22-28 price gap)
- **Differentiation:** Pre-cut patchwork squares, kid-safe quilting needles, step-by-step booklet, tiered difficulty (3 mini quilts: easy/medium/hard)
- **Target keywords:** "quilting kit for kids," "patchwork sewing kit," "quilt kit for beginners kids"
- **Higher differentiation** — completely uncontested sub-niche

**Must-Have Features (either path):**
- Premium materials (thick felt/fleece — NOT thin)
- Gift-ready packaging (premium box, gift tag)
- Clear age guidance with difficulty tiers
- Visual step-by-step instruction booklet
- "Screen-free" in title/bullets
