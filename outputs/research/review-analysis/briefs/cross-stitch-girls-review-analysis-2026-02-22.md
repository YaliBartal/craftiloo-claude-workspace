## Executive Summary

Craftiloo's Cross Stitch Girls Kit is in a **healthy position** with 82% satisfaction and strong praise for ease of use, cute designs, and gift appeal. The main vulnerability is **material quality complaints** (12% of reviews) — specifically thread tangling and hoop-to-fabric fit issues — which KRAFUN has completely avoided. KRAFUN is the real competitive threat at 4.54 avg rating vs our 4.4, while Pllieay (3.66) and Caydo (3.84) struggle with far worse quality issues.

**Sentiment Health:** HEALTHY
**Top Finding:** KRAFUN achieves 90% satisfaction with zero material quality complaints — their felt keyring format may sidestep the hoop/fabric fit issues that plague our kit and Pllieay's
**Most Urgent Action:** Address hoop/fabric fit issue (4 complaints) — either increase hoop size or adjust fabric weight. This is the most fixable product-level complaint.
