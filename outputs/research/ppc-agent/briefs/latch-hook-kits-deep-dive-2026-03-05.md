## Structural Issues

### 1. Missing PP (Product Page) Modifiers — HIGH IMPACT
None of the 12 campaigns have PP modifiers. Detail Page placement delivers 19.6% ACoS (best of all placements) but gets only 6% of spend. Adding PP modifiers would capture more of this efficient traffic.

### 2. Concentration Risk — HIGH IMPACT
48.4% of spend and 53.2% of sales come from one keyword ("latch hook kit for kids"). If this keyword's performance drops or competition increases, the entire portfolio takes a hit.

### 3. Rank Erosion on Root Keywords — HIGH IMPACT
"latch hook kit" and "latch hook" — combined 30K+ SV — have dropped off page 3+. The paused "latch hook root TOS" campaign ($25/d, TOS 164%) was likely supporting these ranks. Without PPC support, organic rank has collapsed.

### 4. Low-Activity Campaigns — MEDIUM IMPACT
4 campaigns generated <$5 each in 30 days. SK "latch hook kits for beginners" has $0 spend. Either bids are too low to compete, or the keywords need different match types.

### 5. "Rug" Keyword Family Underperforming — MEDIUM IMPACT
MK rug root exact TOS push: 82.4% ACoS, SK hook rug kits for kids: 128.1% ACoS. The "rug" keyword cluster converts poorly for a pencil case product. This makes sense — users searching "rug" want actual rugs, not pencil cases.
