## Why This Rebuild

This portfolio has been struggling for over a year. The cycle has been: enable campaigns → they bleed → pause them → rank drops → re-enable → bleed again. The product itself is strong — it wins at Christmas and has proven it can convert on the right keywords. The problem was never the product; it was portfolio architecture.

**What was wrong:**
1. **14 campaigns accumulated over a year** with overlapping targeting and no coherent structure
2. **Broad campaigns carried 29%+ of spend** at 52.8% ACoS — imprecise matching drove up waste
3. **Discovery campaigns (Auto, MK Broad) had zero negatives** — every irrelevant click bled money
4. **SK campaigns were over-bid** — 161-181% TOS modifiers on keywords that don't need top-of-search dominance
5. **Wrong-ASIN and dormant campaigns** still had active budgets ($25/day wasted)
6. **Listing was broken until Mar 3** — 10% ranking juice, wrong product in title, 2-word description. PPC was trying to compensate for a listing that couldn't convert. Now the listing is fixed.

**The core insight:** Revenue should come from precision (SK Exact campaigns), not from broad fishing nets. Every keyword that proves it converts should get its own SK campaign. Broad campaigns are for discovery only — not revenue.

---

## The Strategic Framework

### Portfolio Architecture Principle
```
SK Exact campaigns  = Revenue engines  (60-70% of spend)
Auto + MK broad     = Discovery only   (15-20% of spend)
Defence             = Brand protection  (5-10% of spend)
Everything else     = Paused or phased out
```

### The Flywheel (ongoing optimization cycle)
```
Auto/MK Broad discovers terms
        ↓
Every 2 weeks: harvest search terms
        ↓
Winners (ACoS <35%, 2+ orders) → Promote to new SK campaign
Losers (0 orders, 20+ clicks) → Negate in Auto/MK broad
        ↓
SK campaigns that hit <25% ACoS → Increase budget 20%
SK campaigns still >45% ACoS after 30d → Lower bid 15%
        ↓
Portfolio gets more precise over time
```

### Seasonal Strategy
- **Off-season (Jan-Aug):** Run lean at ~$50/day. Focus on profitability and organic rank building.
- **Pre-season (Sep):** Scale SK budgets 2x. Add PT competitor campaigns. Push TOS on hero keywords.
- **Peak season (Oct-Dec):** Scale to $70-100/day. This is when the product prints money — invest accordingly.
- **Post-season (Jan):** Drop back to lean. Harvest Q4 search term data for new SK campaigns.
