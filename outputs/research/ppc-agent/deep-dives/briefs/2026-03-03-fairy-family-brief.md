1. **Rank erosion across the board.** 28-day net -124 positions, hero keyword "sewing kit for kids" dropped from #7 to #14. Root cause: likely increased competition + insufficient TOS aggression on hero keyword campaign ($0.34 base bid, 261% TOS = $1.23 effective — may be outbid at TOS).

2. **900% TOS on auto campaigns is burning money.** Two auto campaigns (fairy TOS + ballerina TOS) have 10x TOS multipliers. Auto campaigns don't control which keyword gets TOS — Amazon could be placing irrelevant searches at 10x bid premium. This is an anti-pattern that should never be used.

3. **Highest-budget SK missing TOS.** "sewing kit for kids ages 8-12" at $30/day has zero TOS modifier. This keyword (3,420 SV) is dropping (#4→#8) and the campaign isn't even pushing for top-of-search placement. Major structural miss.

5. **Multi-ASIN complexity without clear separation.** 5 ASINs in one portfolio, but campaigns don't clearly separate which ASIN they serve. Ballerina has dedicated campaigns but shares portfolio ACoS. Hard to optimize when ASINs have different margin profiles.

6. **Budget underutilization at 25%.** $322/day total budget but only $81/day actual spend. Campaigns are demand-constrained, not budget-constrained — adding budget won't help, but bid/TOS adjustments could capture more impressions.

7. **No search term data available.** Amazon Ads API reports stuck in PENDING. Cannot perform search term harvesting, negation analysis, or cannibalization detection. This is a known Amazon-side issue but leaves a significant blind spot.
