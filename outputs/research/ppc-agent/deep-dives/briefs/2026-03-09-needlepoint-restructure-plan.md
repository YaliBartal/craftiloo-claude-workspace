## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1: Consolidate (This Week — Mar 10-14)
**Goal:** Cut waste spend, keep what works.

| # | Action | Campaign | What | Impact |
|---|--------|----------|------|--------|
| 1 | KEYWORD_PAUSE | MK Broad | Pause "needlepoint kit" BROAD ($166/mo, 66% ACoS) | -$166/mo waste |
| 2 | KEYWORD_PAUSE | MK Broad | Pause "needlepoint kits" BROAD ($52/mo, 146% ACoS) | -$52/mo waste |
| 3 | CAMPAIGN_PAUSE | SK beginners | Pause (1.7% utilization, 59% ACoS, chasing generic "beginners") | -$53/mo |
| 4 | CAMPAIGN_PAUSE | SK kids needlepoint | Pause (0% utilization, 68% ACoS) | -$37/mo |
| 5 | CAMPAIGN_PAUSE | SK kit for kids | Pause (0% utilization, 86% ACoS) | -$17/mo |
| 6 | BUDGET_DECREASE | Auto | $5/day → $3/day (discovery-only role) | -$60/mo |

**Estimated monthly savings: ~$385/month**
**Remaining active:** MK Broad (kids needlepoint keyword only) + Auto ($3/d) + SK Pouch Kit + PT

### Phase 2: Listing Fix (Mar 10-21)
**Goal:** Double CVR from 5% → 10%.

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | Run listing-optimizer on B09HVDNFMR (Cat) + B09HVSLBS6 (Dog) |

**Why this matters:** If CVR doubles from 5% to 10%, the same $325/month spend produces ~50-60 orders instead of 25-30. ACoS drops to ~15-18%. The portfolio becomes genuinely profitable.

### Phase 3: Re-assess (Mar 23+)
