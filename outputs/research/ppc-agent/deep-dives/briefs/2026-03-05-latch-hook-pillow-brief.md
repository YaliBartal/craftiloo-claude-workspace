## Structural Issues Summary

### Issue 1: PT Campaign Overspend (36.6% of budget, 51.4% ACoS)
**Why:** PT is the #1 spender at $498/30d but delivers 51.4% ACoS. Target b06xrrn4vl alone cost $263 at 55.4% ACoS. Plus we're self-targeting our own ASIN at 95.9% ACoS.
**Impact:** $498 at 51.4% means ~$245 is waste above 3.5x ROAS. PT should be 5-10% of spend, not 36.6%.
**Fix:** (a) Negate self-ASIN target, (b) reduce bids on underperforming targets, (c) add top competitor B0DJQPGDMQ.

### Issue 2: Under-spending SK Campaigns
**Why:** 7 of 10 campaigns spent <$20 each over 30 days. Missing PP/ROS modifiers mean they can only compete for TOS placements at base bids ($0.23-$0.54). Amazon needs $0.38-$0.74 for non-TOS auctions.
**Impact:** SK campaigns should be 40%+ of spend but are only 25.2%. The efficient SK latch hook kit for kids (28.7% ACoS) is capped because it's the only SK with PP/ROS modifiers.
**Fix:** Add PP/ROS modifiers to all SK and MK campaigns to unlock non-TOS placements.

### Issue 3: Auto Campaign Bleeding
**Why:** 72.4% ACoS on $72 spend. 101 unique search terms but only 4 orders. High volume of low-quality discovery traffic.
**Impact:** $72 with only $100 in sales means -$47 net loss from Auto (before COGS).
**Fix:** Reduce Auto default bid from $0.23 to lower level, or reduce TOS modifier.

### Issue 4: Missing SK for "latch hook pillow kit"
**Why:** The most efficient converting term (15.4% ACoS, 14 orders) is only captured by MK Broad. The SK pillow kit campaign exists but barely spends ($9/30d).
**Impact:** MK Broad is capturing this keyword at $0.72 CPC when a dedicated SK at higher TOS could scale it further.
**Fix:** Fix the SK pillow kit campaign (add PP/ROS, potentially increase bid/TOS).

### Issue 5: Self-Targeting Waste
**Why:** B0F8R652FX is both our hero ASIN AND a PT target. We're paying to advertise on our own product page.
**Impact:** $19 wasted at 95.9% ACoS over 30 days.
**Fix:** Negative-target our own ASIN in the PT campaign.

### Issue 6: "Adults" Keyword Waste
**Why:** Multiple "adults" search terms (latch hook pillow kits for adults, latch hook kits for adults pillow, etc.) generate clicks but 0 orders. Product is designed for kids/families.
**Impact:** $11.90 wasted over 30 days (small but preventable and growing).
**Fix:** Add "adults" as negative phrase to Auto and MK Broad.
