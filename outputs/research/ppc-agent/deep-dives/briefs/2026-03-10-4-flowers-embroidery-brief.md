## Executive Summary

**Health Score: 3/10** — Portfolio is in critical condition. Spending $1,240/30d at 69.8% ACoS (target 30%), meaning roughly **$560/month in wasted ad spend**. Only 2 of 9 enabled campaigns are within striking distance of target. The portfolio suffers from three systemic problems:

1. **No negative keyword hygiene** — MK Broad and Auto have ZERO negatives, bleeding on irrelevant/unqualified traffic
2. **Structural over-reliance on broad matching** — MK broad ultra targeted (52.8% ACoS) accounts for 29% of spend but ACoS is 22.8pp above target
3. **Multiple bleeding campaigns** — 5 of 9 enabled campaigns have ACoS >80% or zero sales
4. **Rank erosion continues** — "embroidery kit for beginners" (103K SV) fell from rank 27 to 101. Only 0-1 keywords in top 10 out of 67 tracked.
5. **Bright spot:** SK beginners embroidery kit for adults (43.1% ACoS, 19 orders) and Defence brand KW (6.6% ACoS, 4 orders) prove the product can convert when targeted correctly.

**Phase 1 (Mar 2) and Phase 2 (Mar 3) interventions are showing mixed results.** ACoS improved from 67.9% → 48.0% (7d basis) but remains double the target. Listing rewrite needs more time to impact organic rank.

## Structural Issues Summary

1. **SK campaign dominance is absent.** Only 21% of spend goes to precise SK campaigns vs 29% to MK broad ultra targeted. This means most traffic is loosely matched, driving up ACoS.

2. **Zero negative keywords on 2 of 9 enabled campaigns** (MK Broad TOS, SPA Auto). Every click on these campaigns is unfiltered — guaranteed waste on irrelevant terms.

3. **Wrong-ASIN campaign still enabled.** B0BWSGRTB1 SPM MP brand ASINs ($15/30d, $0 sales) targets a completely different product. Pure waste.

4. **SK embroidery starter kit is hemorrhaging.** 250.9% ACoS, 1 order in 30 days, $17/day budget. This campaign alone wastes ~$50/month.

5. **Defence brand KW is massively underutilized.** 6.6% ACoS STAR performer with $30/day budget but only spending $0.20/day. Either bid is too low to win auctions or targeting is too narrow.

6. **Shield campaign at 82% ACoS** is supposed to protect brand searches, not bleed money. Needs bid/TOS reduction.

7. **Rank erosion on hero keywords** continues despite listing rewrite. "Embroidery kit for beginners" at rank 42 (was 27) — insufficient PPC support for organic rank recovery.

8. **Missing SK campaign for "embroidery kits for adults"** (33K SV) — the #2 converting search term by orders but no dedicated SK campaign exists.
